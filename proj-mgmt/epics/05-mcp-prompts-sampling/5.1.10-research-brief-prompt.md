# 5.1.10: `research_brief` prompt

**Status:** BACKLOG
**Type:** Story
**Feature:** [5.1: Prompt Templates](./5.1-prompt-templates.md)
**Tags:** `+prompts` `+research`

---

As an attorney or intern,
I want a prompt that compiles a research brief on a legal topic with relevant precedents,
So that I have a structured research foundation for a matter.

- [ ] Arguments: `topic`, `jurisdiction`, `caseId?`
- [ ] Fetch relevant research memos and embed
- [ ] Structure: issue, relevant law, precedents, analysis, conclusion

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Brief follows IRAC structure (Issue, Rule, Analysis, Conclusion)
- [ ] Cites specific research memos found in the database
