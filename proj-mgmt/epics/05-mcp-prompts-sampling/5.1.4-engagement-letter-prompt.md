As a partner,
I want a prompt that drafts an engagement letter from client intake data,
So that onboarding new clients is fast and consistent.

- [ ] Arguments: `clientId`, `matterDescription`, `feeStructure`
- [ ] Fetch client details and embed in prompt
- [ ] feeStructure drives the fee section (Hourly, Retainer, Contingency)

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Letter includes standard engagement terms, scope, and fee arrangement
- [ ] Client name and contact info are pre-populated from client record
